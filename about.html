About This Portal

This portal aims to help GTU students access complete study materials – lecture notes, GTU books, previous year question papers, videos, playlists, and unit-wise resources. It is designed to keep everything in one place, clean and easy to use.

Reference Websites We Use

We collect and organize verified GTU materials from trusted educational sources:

  • GTU Official Website: Syllabus PDFs, circulars, academic calendar, and official notifications.
  • GTU Study Mates: Unit-wise notes and student-friendly breakdown of concepts.
  • Darshan University Study Material: One of the most accurate and detailed GTU resources for all subjects.
  • GTU Study Material Websites: Additional references collected from secure online sources.

GTU Syllabus Coverage

We provide:

  • Full semester-wise GTU syllabus
  • Unit-wise material mapped to the official syllabus
  • Year-wise paper patterns for better exam preparation
  • PYQ (Previous Year Questions) with chapter mapping

Goal of This Project

The goal is to provide **a complete study portal for GTU students**, built by students, for students. This website keeps all materials organized, verified, and accessible with clean UI and NO ads or complexity.
